Gender: The student's gender identity (male or female).
Race/ethnicity: An identifier for the race or ethnicity of the student.
Parental education level: The maximum level of education attained by the student's parents, such as a bachelor's, master's, associate's, or college degree.
Lunch: The student's lunch program enrolment, which may indicate their socioeconomic status (standard, free/reduced, etc.).
Test preparation course: This indicates whether or not the student has finished (completed or none at all) a test preparation course.
Math score: A number that expresses how well a student performed in mathematics.
Reading score: A number that represents how well a student reads.
Writing score: A tally of numbers representing the student's writing proficiency.
